Governance & Democracy

Good Governance strengthens the fundamental building blocks of democracy. Our Good Governance programs focus on the Principles of Democracy, Solutions to the Crisis of Democracy, Citizen Engagement, Transparency, and Accountability.

Capstone Practicum

Our capstone practicum comprises practical approaches to problem-solving. It includes case studies and simulation exercises on tackling critical challenges and technology transfer opportunities to address inefficiencies, build a culture of innovation, and make governments more responsive.

Municipal Leadership Development

Investing in developing effective and inclusive leadership provides short- and long-term benefits to local communities. Effective leadership starts with understanding best practices and principles of leadership and how to apply them in the local context.
